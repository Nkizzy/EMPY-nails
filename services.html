Spa Pedicure Details

Empy Spa Pedicure ($50)

Contains a nutrient rich blend of pure essential oils to sweep away dead skin cells and clear congested pores leaving skin clean, fresh, and invigorated. Treat dry calluses and dead skin on the soles of your feet with a callus remover, and finally, we use a massage hammer to lightly massage the legs to promote better circulation.

Includes: 10 mins. Massage + Hot stone massage

Organic Spa Pedicure ($60)

Fruit extract is a rich natural moisturizer that deeply hydrates and nourishes the skin. Gently exfoliate and moisturize to leave the skin feeling silky, smooth, and replenished. Treat dry calluses and dead skin on the soles of your feet with a callus remover, and finally, we use a massage hammer to lightly massage the legs to promote blood circulation.

Includes: 10 minute Massage + Hot stone massage

Deluxe Spa Pedicure ($70)

Soak your feet in a warm jelly to soften your skin. Then, exfoliate with a vanilla sugar scrub. While your nails and cuticles are warmed, we will use a callus removal treatment to remove dry and dead skin on the soles of your feet, and we will gently massage your legs with a massage hammer to promote blood circulation and achieve complete relaxation.

Includes: 15 minute massage + jelly + hot stone massage

Royal Spa Pedicure ($90)

Gold Spa is a symbol of wealth, health, strength, and power. The Egyptians have known about the positive effects of precious metals on the body and soul for over 4000 years, and scientific discoveries about the medicinal properties of gold have provided great inspiration for spa treatments. We treat the dry and dead skin on the soles of your feet with a callus remover, then wrap them with warm wax in hot water, and finally use a massage hammer to boost circulation. Let the body and mind achieve complete relaxation.

Includes: 25 minute massage, hot towel wrap + paraffin wrap + hot stone massage
